Rihla – Delete your account

Rihla is published by STM. You can request that your account and associated data are deleted in either of the following ways.

Option 1: In the Rihla app

  1. Open the Rihla app.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon).
  3. Tap Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account and follow the prompts.

This permanently deletes your account and associated data from our systems.

Option 2: By email

If you cannot use the app, email us and we will process your deletion request:

stm@almawali.com

Include your account email address so we can identify your account. We will confirm once the deletion is complete.
